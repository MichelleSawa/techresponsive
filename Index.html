Michelle Sawa's Past Projects

Using the human-centered design process as my guide, I conduct qualitative research to gather insights and humanize brands with words and customer-focused copy.

There are few things I enjoy more than talking to people and hearing their stories. My passion for people led me to fields with communication and connection at the core—writing, marketing, teaching, and comedy.

I've worked at email marketing and communication firms, taught reading intervention at a charter school, written a marketing book called Car Dog Millionaire, studied improvisation at Second City, and even dabbled in stand-up comedy in Brazil. Now, I've landed in the IT world and am helping Liongard enable insight through automation.

Past Projects

Teaching Teens Human-Centered Design

As a former classroom teacher, I still have great passion for teaching. I decided to go back to the classroom to teach my former students about human-centered design. A Prototype Program: Building a Creative Outlet for Students.

OWLFA Financial Advisors

Owlfa Financial Advisors hired me to help them reach their audience: teachers trying to navigate retirement savings. I conducted a qualitative research study and wrote human-focused copy which can be seen on their current website.

Writing a Marketing Book for Car Dealers

In 2015, I co-wrote a marketing book with one of my clients. The funniest part about this project was the fact that I knew close to nothing about cars. I recapped the experience on Medium: How to Write a Book on a Topic You Know Nothing About. You can find Car Dog Millionaire on Amazon. It has lots of 5-star reviews, which makes you wonder if it’s fake! ;)
